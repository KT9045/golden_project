What to expect?

Self-learning!

Small class sizes of 10 students maximum. Course booking – from initial advice to enrolment. Free coffee & tea! A relaxed and friendly atmosphere where every student is known by name

Incredible moments!

Accommodation, transport, books, a new language…. if you’re coming to learn English to Lviv there is a lot to organise. We are pleased to help our students in whatever way we can to make their stay as enjoyable and productive as possible.

Smart life-style!

Our social programme of events runs all year round and consists of afternoon, evening and weekend activities. The programme is designed to allow you to take advantage of Lviv fantastic range of cultural activities, whilst making friends and improving your English at the same time.
